Montreal Canadiens goaltender Carey Price reveals the unimaginable pressure he faced playing in the city

Carey Price, the goaltender for the Montreal Canadiens, has spoken about the pressure of playing in Montreal in an interview with La Poche Bleue. Price, who has played for the Canadiens for 15 seasons, described Montreal as a "weakness" for players and emphasized the importance of mental health resources for players. He also spoke about the importance of being able to talk about one's struggles rather than keeping them internalized, especially if there is no one to confide in.
